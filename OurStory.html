Our Story
Our mission is to promote environmentally friendly tote bags that will help the environment by reducing the consumption of disposable plastic bags. Our vision is to limit the consumption of plastic bags, and promote sustainable tote bags as a great alternative to plastic bags that are harmful to the environment. To make our tote bags sustainable and eco-friendly, we plan to use recycled cotton which is considered as one of the most sustainable types of fabric.
